Life of Andrew Mendelsohn

Andrew Mendelsohn (right, above) is a sophomore at Tufts University. He is studying for a Bachelor's of the Arts in Computer Science. Andrew grew up in Lexington, MA and graduated from Lexington High School where he played guitar in the LHS Jazz Ensemble. Andrew also spent a summer at the Berklee College of music and is a reserve guitar player for the Beantown Swing Orchestra.
